Getting started in Minesweeper

The goal of the game is to flag every tile that has a mine, while revealing every other tile. Tiles can be revealed by left-clicking on them. Flags can be added or removed by right-clicking on the tile.
When you reveal a tile next to a mine (diagonals count), the number of adjacent mines is displayed on the tile. Use this information to deduce which tiles the mines must be on.
Revealing a tile with no adjacent mines will automatically reveal all surrounding tiles and every other "empty" tile adjacent to that one. Large areas can be uncovered this way.
If you have placed as many flags next to a tile as there are adjacent mines, you can double-click on the tile to automatically reveal any non-flagged adjacent tiles. This can speed your game up considerably.
Your first click is "protected". It is impossible to hit a mine on the first tile you reveal.
Sometimes completing a game will require one or more guesses. Good luck!
